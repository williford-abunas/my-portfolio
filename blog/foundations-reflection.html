Foundations Reflections

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

  • I learned I can achieve something if I put my mind into it.
  • I've learned about some of my destructive habits
  • I learned that it's ok to be wrong when trying to learn

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Values will help build relationships with ourselves and others. Empathy will help in understanding one's colleagues and working with them. It will also help when you are trying to build a solution for a customer's problem. Self-awareness will help keep one's mental well - being in a healthy state.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

I like how it focuses on the human side of programming and how the brain works. What surprised me the most is the amount of change or mindset that I gained in such a short time. I haven't applied everything yet but knowing and dipping my toes in praciticing strategies like reflection, empathy through listening/looping, as well as timeboxing made me realize their importance and practicability.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Timeboxing for me most of the time as well as getting help when stucked.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

I think core learning is important because of the fact that we will be dealing with people in the workplace. Having positive values will help not only a person to grow but also those people around.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

Definitely not a waste of time. I believe without studying about ourselves and how we relate to others would make us robots instead of persons.
